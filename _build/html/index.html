MS: Unsupervised Identification of Functional Domains on Spatially Resolved Brain Cell Atlas¶

Introduction¶

MS is designed for automatically tissue structure identifications on spatially resolved single cell data, for example, STARmap, MERFISH, slide-seq, etc.

MS support both multi-slice and single-slice analysis. MS’s best advance is to analyze very large scale datasets. MS can finish the task within an hour to process an atlas-scale datasets containing 1 million cells.

MS can also support tissue visualizations by embedding the MS-representation into a color space.

MS support 3D datasets.
